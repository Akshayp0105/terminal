State Bank of India (SBI)

Criteria:

  • Minimum age: 21 years
  • Maximum age at loan maturity: 65 years
  • Stable employment or business

Documents Required:

  • Identity proof (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, Passport, etc.)
  • Address proof (Utility bill, Ration card, Passport, etc.)
  • Income proof (Salary slips, Form 16, IT returns)
  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Passport-sized photographs

General Process for Car Loan:

  1. Application: Apply online or visit the bank branch with required documents.
  2. Verification: The bank verifies the documents and assesses eligibility.
  3. Sanction: Based on eligibility, the loan is sanctioned.
  4. Disbursement: The loan amount is disbursed directly to the car dealer.
