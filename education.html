State Bank of India (SBI)

Criteria:

  • Indian national
  • Secured admission to a recognized course in India or abroad
  • Courses: Graduation, post-graduation, professional, and technical courses

Documents Required:

  • Admission letter from the institution
  • Fee structure of the course
  • Mark sheets of previous qualifying examinations
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, Passport, etc.)
  • Address proof (Utility bill, Ration card, Passport, etc.)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Income proof of parents/guardians (Salary slips, IT returns)

General Process for Educational Loan:

  1. Application: Apply online or visit the bank branch with the required documents.
  2. Verification: The bank verifies the documents and assesses eligibility.
  3. Sanction: Based on eligibility, the loan is sanctioned.
  4. Disbursement: The loan amount is disbursed directly to the educational institution.
